2p.

Description


Plaza Kings are located on floors 5-19 and provide elegant accommodations with a comfortable sitting area. Each of these exceptional New York City hotel rooms features a bathroom finished with inlaid "earth stone" mosaics in floral motifs, with a bathtub as well as separate shower. Bathroom details include 24-carat gold plated Sherle Wagner faucets and accessories, and hand crafted solid white marble vanities.
